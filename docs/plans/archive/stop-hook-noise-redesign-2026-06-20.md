## Goal
The operator's complaint, in his words: the Stop hooks at the end of a turn force
me to re-produce output, which loops without adding information, and the
end-of-session reports degrade and repeat. Root cause: three Stop gates
(PR-health, customer-facing-review, completion-criteria) default to **block** — so
when a required section is absent the hook blocks the turn-end and I re-emit with
it appended. That verifies *text was pasted*, not that *work finished*, and it
loops. Fix: flip their default to **warn** — they surface once and never block.
Honest completion stays governed by the DONE/PAUSING/BLOCKED marker
(`continuation-enforcer`), which keeps pause/ask first-class (a turn that pauses to
ask the operator a question must never require a completion artifact — the
operator's own design insight, 2026-06-20).

## User-facing Outcome
The operator gets silence while work runs and one clean message per ending — a
DONE report, or a clear question stating exactly what he needs to provide — with
no forced re-paste loops. Pausing to ask is never blocked.

## Scope
- IN: flip the default block→warn in `pr-health-snapshot-gate.sh`,
  `customer-facing-review-gate.sh`, `completion-criteria-gate.sh`; install live.
- OUT (kept block — these are the *useful* enforcement the operator wants):
  `continuation-enforcer.sh` (the DONE/PAUSING/BLOCKED marker) and
  `narrate-and-wait-gate.sh` (blocks aimless permission-seeking stops = the
  "don't stop mid-run" directive). OUT (next, not this plan): `decision-context-gate`
  (a section-forcer that should also warn) and a rule codifying the one-clean-report
  format. The env/file `block` override is preserved on all three.
